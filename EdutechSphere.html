Executive Lead ME-RIISE FOUNDATION (2024-2025)

MOTTO - "We Teach to Learn, Share to Grow."

"EduTechSphere was founded with the goal of developing essential skills among individuals, fostering a culture of continuous learning, and promoting the sharing of knowledge to empower growth and innovation."

Vision:

"To lead the way in skill development through technology-driven education, empowering individuals and institutions with hands-on learning that drives innovation and practical expertise."

Mission:

"At EduTechSphere, we are committed to continuous learning, skill development, and knowledge sharing. Our mission is to help individuals and organizations enhance their technical capabilities through immersive, hands-on sessions and real-world applications."
